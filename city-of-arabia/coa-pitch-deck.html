Philosophy
Between Dream and Reality.
The Register
Magical realism. Not strict documentary, not pure fantasy. Sound exists in the space where both feel true at once.
The Frontier
Sound design must inhabit the liminal space between the tangible and the dreamlike. Every texture carries both weight and wonder.
The Approach
Grounded enough to groove. Weightless enough to float. The balance is the craft.

03 / 11
Sonic Territory
A Sonic Geography
01
Visual Territory
A purely Mediterranean aesthetic transposed to a luxurious desert setting. Sun-drenched architecture, warm terracotta, lush botanical contrasts.
02
Sonic Moodboard
Balearic indie, sun-drenched grooves and warm analog production. A rhythm that never disappears, only shifts intensity. Refs: Poolside, Khruangbin, Bonobo, Tycho, Portishead.
03
Organic Instrumentation
Clean electric guitar with spring reverb, warm Rhodes piano, immersive analog pads. Every instrument chosen for warmth and tactile presence.
04
Tactile Percussion
Soft brushed drums and warm synth bass anchoring a laid-back groove. Percussion present but never heavy, the pulse of a Mediterranean afternoon.
04 / 11
Translation
Translating Image
into Sound
Visual Stimulus
Light Flares
Visual transitions between spaces and states of consciousness.
Botanical Growth
The central character. Plants, vines and flowers blooming progressively throughout the film.
Sonic Response
Swells & Shimmers
A recurring sonic wash (synthesized texture) that cleanses between scenes. The audible signature of transition.
Harmonic Accumulation
Layering complexity. Musical and structural density grows organically in parallel with the flora.

06 / 11
The Echo
The Final Echo
"Was it a dream, or was it real?"
The Loop
Circular structure. The film opens and closes with the exact same image. The score mirrors this return.
The Ambiguous Resolution
The score returns to the starting point but carries one extra note. A harmony that refuses to fully resolve. The gap in the circle.

Execution
Action Plan
PHASE 01
Composition &
Arrangement
Build the dynamic skeleton for the 5-act arc (45s).
Develop harmonic accumulation progression to mirror botanical growth.
Build core sound palette: clean electric guitar, warm synth bass, Rhodes piano, soft brushed drums.
PHASE 02
Sound Design &
Textures
Synthesize bespoke shimmers and washes (synthesized textures) for light flare transitions.
Build the organic ambience for Act 1 and design the final echo for Act 5.
PHASE 03
Mixing &
Finalization
Ensure the balearic warmth and sun-drenched quality throughout.
Precisely balance the frontier between organic real elements and ethereal processing (digital effects).
